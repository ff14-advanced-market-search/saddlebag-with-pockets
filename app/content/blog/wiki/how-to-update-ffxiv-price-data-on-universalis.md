We get our data from [Universalis](https://universalis.app) and just changing prices does not update universalis.

To update universalis you must have [DalamudPlugins](https://dalamud.dev/faq/getting-started/) or another universalis collector installed.

The data only updates when you go to the marketboard and look up the prices. It does not update if you just change prices in your retainers.

If you have issues or questions ask about it in the [Universalis Discord](https://discord.gg/TdXF3RfkBe)

[Read the wiki on how data is updated here](https://github.com/Universalis-FFXIV/guides/wiki/How-to-help-update-the-data-on-Universalis%3F)
