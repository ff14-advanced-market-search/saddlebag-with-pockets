# Home Server Configurations

These configurations are accessed through the same step

1. [Head to our homepage](https://saddlebagexchange.com/)
2. Click on the cog circled in red below on the upper right of your screen.

![Screenshot 2023-02-06 230916](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/124644331/217146224-c79eefba-9376-44bb-93af-3efc057181d3.png)

- FFXIV World Selection
- WoW Home Realm Selection
- Website Theme

## \***\*FFXIV World Selection\*\***

Upon FFXIV World Selection the two options highlighted: Data Center & World

### Data Center & World

1. Click on "Aether" the default data center to view selected servers that change the marketplace your queries are run against.
   ![Screenshot 2023-02-06 231818](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/124644331/217147247-c706a463-9ce1-4c2f-acd5-032f141d8e42.png)

2. Choose between North American Data Centers, European Data Centers, or Oceanic Data Centers & make world option.

(**NOTE: Each data center offers a variety of World options. Click through each to find your selection.**
)
![Screenshot 2023-02-06 232107](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/124644331/217147575-5d1a14f3-2b16-4f1c-abd3-6837594635d2.png)

![Screenshot 2023-02-06 232553](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/124644331/217148163-993cb9c4-0fc7-4991-83b3-fa673afcc679.png)

4. Your settings will be automatically updated.
